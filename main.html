Summary

"Dreamscape" is an exhilarating sci-fi adventure that takes the audience on a journey through the dreams and fears of its characters. The story follows a group of individuals with the unique ability to enter and manipulate dreams. As they delve into the subconscious mind, they uncover hidden secrets, confront inner demons, and race against time to prevent a global catastrophe.

Main Actors

  • John Marshall as Agent Daniel Thorn
  • Lila Williams as Sarah Weaver
